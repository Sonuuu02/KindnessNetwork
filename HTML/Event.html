Ride For Sick Kids WA 2024

Introducing Ride For Sick Kids WA 2024!

WA’s Ride for Sick Kids has raised over $3.5 million over the last 11 years. Now in its twelfth year, 2024 will see a peloton of dedicated riders embark on a journey through the South West from 1-5 May to raise funds for WA families with sick kids. The annual four-day ride will include visits to country communities that are reliant on RMHC WA services due to the impacts of a child’s illness. Your support towards Ride For Sick Kids WA ensures RMHC WA can continue to be there for WA families when they need it most.
