Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) are private entities pivotal in fostering development across local, national, and international realms through the mobilization of indigenous communities. Functioning as citizen advocates, NGOs heighten awareness and wield influence over policies, encompassing a diverse spectrum of entities such as independent cooperatives, community associations, societies, and various other affiliations.
The Foundation for Social Change and Inclusion is dedicated to fostering social development and facilitating the integration of marginalized individuals, groups, and communities within Bulgaria.
Our mission is to disrupt the relentless cycle of poverty and social alienation, instilling renewed hope for a brighter tomorrow. We staunchly advocate for the fundamental right of every individual to access resources and opportunities, enabling them to flourish with dignity and actively contribute to the fabric of our society.
