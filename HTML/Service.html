Teach your kids about giving

Giving unwanted toys to those less fortunate, smiling at someone on the street, holding doors open for people or visiting a sick relative is still charity because it warms someone's heart. You can introduce kiddos to different charities to show how any size of donation can go a long way in helping people. If your child received gifted money on their birthday or a holiday, you can approach them about giving some to a cause they want to support.
